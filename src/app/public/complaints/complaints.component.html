Frequently Asked Questions

What is Minimart?

MINIMART (Mobile App) is Malaysia's most convenient food and grocery ordering site via mobile, connecting people with the best groceries shops around them, in Kuala Lumpur, Petaling Jaya, Ipoh, Melaka, and many more Malaysian cities! You can find everything from groceries, food, beauty products, jewelry, electronics, fresh vegetables, dairy products, cooking gas, fresh milk, Indian homemade products, bakery items, Indian species many more.

How to contact Minimart team?

You can reach our team via phone call or whatsapp (+60 10 544 9974) Monday to Sunday - 9.00am to 9.00pm, including Public Holidays.

How to change the delivery address?

You can change the delivery address at any time in the mobile app before you placed the order. If you want to change the delivery address after you placed the order, you can reach the Minimart team via phone call or WhatsApp (+60 10 544 9974).

How do I cancel my order?

You can see the cancel order option at order history in the profile section. Cancel option may be disabled once the shop keeper acceptetd the order. You can contact shop (Shop keeper number can see at history section or email) or Minimart customer support any time (+60105449974)

What is the minimum order for delivery?

There is no minimum order for delivery. You can order food and groceries from RM1. There are few shops which resticted to min RM 50 for their shop orders.

How much is the delivery cost?

Its depends on the the distance, weight and the time. We may do free delivery on festival seasons. Generally we charge the delivery fee RM1 for 1KM (This is applicapable after 5KM)

Is Minimart an ecommerce?

Minimart e-commerce online grocery platform is the first and fastest online Indian grocery and food network to delliver food, groceries across klang velley in Malaysia.

How much is the service fee?

Our service fee starts from RM5. It varies based on store, delivery distance and selected delivery slot.

What should I do if the goods received are not as per my order?

For the wrong/damage/faulty products that are caused by our store people, you can call the Minimart customer service (+6010 544 9974) and raise the complaint. Our Customer Service will contact you to reschedule another delivery with free delivery cost. We apologize for any inconvenience caused.

How soon can I receive the order?

The earliest you can receive your order is the same working day from the time you make your purchase, subject to available delivery slots.

What happens if something is out of stock?

When something is out of stock at the shop, we do our best to replace it with a similar item. The shop keeper should call to customer and inform the same. Based on the customer confirmation we will update the item.

What are the payment methods available?

Currently we have cash on delivery option for the customers. Few shops may ask customers to transfer money to their company account. We are integrating online banking and credit/debit card payment soon.!

What kind of products do you sell?

At Minimart mobile app, you can choose from over thousands products spread across various categories such as fresh meats and vegetables, food essentials, bakery, beauty and health appliances and much more. Since our mobile app is still new, we work hard to make your shopping experience wonderful. Please let us know via message or email if we missed out any of your daily needs.

Can I purchase more items at a time?

To help ensure a smooth delivery process and to protect the safety of our Riders who are unable to handle large loads on their motorcycles, there are quantity limits for certain items on Minimart. We can deliver on bikes up to 12 kg weight and book car up to 50 kg weight.
